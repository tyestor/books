## Adjudications (criterion 10)

- **Mistborn, Era One**: the paradigm pass — the author divided the universe into discrete eras, each a self-contained unit with its own author-set order. Era One concludes its arcs; Era Two is independent; Cosmere links are optional.
- **World of the Five Gods**: the complete author-defined unit of three novels in author-set order — Paladin of Souls directly continues The Curse of Chalion; The Hallowed Hunt stands alone within the unit. Penric novellas are optional.
- **Memory, Sorrow, and Thorn / The First Law / Powder Mage**: each has sequel series that are complete and well-regarded, so the full unit passes and the author-defined entry trilogy stands on its own.
- **The Merlin Trilogy**: completes Merlin's arc; The Wicked Day is an optional, well-regarded companion volume.
- **The Witcher**: author-set sequence (two collections, then the saga); concluded. Season of Storms is an optional standalone.
- **Master Li**: a numbered authorial sequence of three closed novels; no dangling arc exists.
